# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a professional, respectful, and productive environment for contributors, maintainers, users, and security researchers.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming, precise, and constructive language.
- Respect different experience levels and areas of expertise.
- Focus reviews and discussions on the work, tradeoffs, and evidence.
- Share context when proposing changes, especially for data, security, and legal-domain behavior.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them promptly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or repeated disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Mishandling secrets, credentials, production data, or personally identifiable information.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or hostile.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in a permanent ban from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. Include relevant links, screenshots, dates, and context when possible. Maintainers will review reports with discretion and avoid unnecessary disclosure.
